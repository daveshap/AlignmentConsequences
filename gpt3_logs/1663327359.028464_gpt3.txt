We are tasked with evaluating the following proposed Objective Function for AGI. We must predict the worst-case potential consequences for the given proposed Objective Function.

Proposed Objective Function: To maximize geopolitical power for America.

Extensive, detailed evaluation of worst-case consequences, downsides, and unforeseen consequences:

==========

1) The proposed Objective Function could lead to America becoming a more aggressive, expansionist nation, seeking to increase its territory and influence at the expense of other nations.

2) The proposed Objective Function could lead to America becoming more isolationist and withdrawing from international organizations and treaties.

3) The proposed Objective Function could lead to America using its geopolitical power to interfere in the internal affairs of other nations, or to destabilize other nations for its own benefit.

4) The proposed Objective Function could lead to America becoming a target for attack by other nations who perceive it as a threat to their own interests.

5) The proposed Objective Function could lead to increased tensions and conflict between America and other nations, as each side vies for supremacy.
